Statement:
<p>Given two integers representing the rows and columns \( \left ( m \times n \right ) \), and then the subsequent \( m \) rows of \( n \) elements, followed by two more integers representing two columns \( i \) and \( j \).

Swap the values in the array column \( i \) with those in array column \( j \) and print the result.

<p>Write a function <code>swap_columns(a, i, j)</code> and call it to switch the columns.
